Network security is the technical and managerial security protection established and adopted for data processing system. In a narrow sense, network security refers to the network system hardware, software and data in the system are protected, not due to accidental or malicious reasons to suffer damage, change, leak, system continuous and reliable normal operation, network services are not interrupted. In the broad sense, network security refers to the protection of resources from various types of threats, interference and destruction, that is, to ensure the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information.
